FAQ’s

Will cleaning disrupt my kitchen operations?

No. We schedule work during off-hours or downtime to avoid interfering with your business operations .

How often should my kitchen hood system be cleaned?

Cleaning frequency depends on usage. High-volume kitchens typically need cleaning every 1–3 months, while low-volume kitchens may only need it semi-annually or annually.

What’s included in a professional hood cleaning service?

We clean the hood, ductwork, and exhaust fan—removing grease and buildup in accordance with NFPA 96 standards.

Do you provide before-and-after photos or documentation?

Yes. We provide full documentation, including photos and a service report for compliance and peace of mind.

Is hood cleaning required by law?

Yes. Most local fire codes and insurance policies require compliance with NFPA 96 standards for hood cleaning .
